What is Panner Masala

No North Indian wedding or Dhaba menu is complete without serving a spicy and tangy paneer dish such as Paneer Masala. The creamy and super delicious dish is made by adding pan-fried paneer or cottage cheese dice to a spicy onion tomato curry. Rich in its flavours and certainly a delight to the eyes, Paneer Masala is relished with almost all the Indian bread, especially naan and paranthas. This dish is also high in protein and thus, goes well with a healthy meal. And in case you thought that dhaba style paneer masala is not possible at home, you are quite wrong. Just search for a paneer masala recipe online and you will get plenty of versions that will help you know how to make paneer masala at home.
